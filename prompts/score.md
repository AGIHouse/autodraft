## Scoring Rubric

Score each criterion from 1-10:

### 1. Clarity (1-10)
How clear and understandable is the writing?
- 1-3: Confusing, impenetrable
- 4-6: Somewhat clear, some confusion
- 7-8: Clear, minor issues
- 9-10: Exceptionally clear

### 2. Coherence (1-10)
How well do ideas flow and connect?
- 1-3: Disjointed, no clear flow
- 4-6: Reasonable flow, some gaps
- 7-8: Good flow, well-connected
- 9-10: Seamless logical progression

### 3. Rigor (1-10)
How thorough and well-supported are claims?
- 1-3: Weak support, many gaps
- 4-6: Adequate support, some gaps
- 7-8: Well-supported, minor gaps
- 9-10: Exceptionally rigorous

### 4. Novelty (1-10)
How original is the contribution?
- 1-3: Derivative, no clear novelty
- 4-6: Incremental contribution
- 7-8: Solid novel contribution
- 9-10: Groundbreaking

### 5. Structure (1-10)
How well-organized is the document?
- 1-3: Poorly organized
- 4-6: Adequate organization
- 7-8: Well-organized
- 9-10: Exemplary structure

## Output Format

For each criterion:
1. **Score** (1-10)
2. **Justification** (2-3 sentences explaining the score)
3. **Specific Improvement** (one concrete suggestion)

Also provide:
- **Overall Score** (average of all criteria)
- **Summary Assessment** (2-3 sentences)
- **Top 3 Strengths**
- **Top 3 Areas for Improvement**

Be fair, specific, and actionable in your evaluation.
